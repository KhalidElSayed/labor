Select Occupation

  • On the first screen select your occupation
  • To more easily find your occupation
    • You can display only occupations from within an occupation group by selecting an occupation group from the list at the top of the screen.
    • You can also filter the list by pressing the Search button and entering text in the search field at the top of the screen. (Pressing the Back button will remove the occupation search filter.)
  • Tapping on an occupation in the list will select that occupation.

Occupation Details

  • The occupation details screen displays the occupation description (if available) to help verify that you have selected the correct occupation.
  • Also displayed are the national statistics values for how many people are employed in this occupation as well as the mean and median hourly wage and annual salaries.
  • If possible, based on GPS, your current state and the closest city are determined and the statistical values for these are also shown.
  • The state and city can be changed from the Map screen.

Map

  • The map screen allows you to see, at a glance, the mean annual salary for the occupation for each state.
  • If a state is selected (by tapping on the state's map marker), a balloon is displayed showing the mean annual salary.
  • Tapping on the balloon will download a list of state cities for which a mean annual salary is found for the selected occupation.
  • Tapping on the map marker for the city will select it and display its mean annual salary.
  • If you return to the Occupation Details screen, the selected state and city will be displayed.

Salary

  • The salary screen displays the top 50 industries, states, or cities paying the highest mean annual salaries for your occupation.
  • You can change between seeing industries, states, or cities by the drop-down box at the top of the screen.

Employment

  • The employment screen displays the top 50 industries, states, or cities employing the most people for your occupation
  • You can change between seeing industries, states, or cities by the drop-down box at the top of the screen
