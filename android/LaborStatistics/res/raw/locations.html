Select Location

  • On the select location screen, you can select either a state or city.
  • To see the available cities for a state, tap on the state and then tap on the balloon that displays the state name
  • When a state or city is selected, a button will appear at the top of the screen.
  • Tapping on this button selects the state or city and moves to the next screen.

Top Occupations

  • The top occupations screen displays the top 50 occupations for the selected location based on highest mean annual salary or employment
  • You can change between sorting on mean annual salary or employment by the drop-down box at the top of the screen

Occupation Details

  • The occupation details screen displays the occupation description (if available).
  • Also displayed are the national statistics values for how many people are employed in this occupation as well as the mean and median hourly wage and annual salaries.
