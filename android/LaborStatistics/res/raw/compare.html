Select Occupation

  • Unless an occupation was already selected on the "Know your occupation screen", you will be prompted to select your occupation on the first screen
  • To more easily find your occupation
    • You can display only occupations from within an occupation group by selecting an occupation group from the list at the top of the screen.
    • You can also filter the list by pressing the Search button and entering text in the search field at the top of the screen. (Pressing the Back button will remove the occupation search filter.)
  • Tapping on an occupation in the list will select that occupation.

Compare Salary

  • The compare salary screen displays the 10th, 25th, Median (50th), 75th, and 90th percentile annual salary values for the selected occupation
  • An explanation of Percentile Wages can be found at What are Percentile Wages?
  • To see how your salary compares with the National, State, and City percentiles, type your salary into the field at the top of the screen and tap the "Plot" button
  • Your calculated percentile should now be displayed below the graph
  • You can change your selected occupation by selecting the Change Occupation menu item
  • On the State and City tabs, your can change the selected location by selecting the Change Location menu item
