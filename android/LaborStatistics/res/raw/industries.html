Select Industry

  • On the first screen select your preferred industry
  • To more easily find your industry you can also filter the list by typing into the Search field at the top of the screen. (Pressing the Back button will remove the industry search filter.)

Top Occupations

  • The top occupations screen displays the top 50 occupations for the selected industry based on highest mean annual salary or employment
  • You can change between sorting on mean annual salary or employment by the drop-down box at the top of the screen

Occupation Details

  • The occupation details screen displays the occupation description (if available).
  • Also displayed are the national statistics values for how many people are employed in this occupation as well as the mean and median hourly wage and annual salaries.
  • If possible, based on GPS, your current state and the closest city are determined and the statistical values for these are also shown.
  • The state and city can be changed on the Map screen of the "Know your occupation" or "Know where you want to work" screens.
